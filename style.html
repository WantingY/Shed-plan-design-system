Featured

Featured text is the sort of important paragraph that might introduce an article or be placed in a hero banner at the top of a page.

Normal

Include one Strong element somewhere in the paragraph and one em element somewhere in the paragraph so we can see them at work.

Teriary

Create styles for and demonstrate smaller tertiary copy, such as might be used to credit the author of an article, in a footer, or for a caption.
